  • Ballistix 3D game pong

    inspired from minigame Ballistix in game "crash bash"

    in this game there are four players, each player is tasked with keeping the ball from passing through it by moving their respective paddles. If a player concedes 15 points, it will be declared void and the player who is able to survive until the end is the winner.

    the ball will be raised once every few seconds with the maximum number of balls in the arena is 5. be careful if the ball passes through the green circle, if the ball passes through the green circle then its speed increases in a few seconds.
